# 👨‍🔧 Support Process

**Support Cases escalated to R&amp;D** are handled by developers of **all R&amp;D teams that own at least one Product Area**. (Learn more about Product Areas [here](https://drive.google.com/open?id=1FY-Xb0V7Szq9QMg_D9ftu3QpECAuMrbh95krVTit96k))

A **subset of developers** from each team fulfills the **Support role** and is responsible in the context of the Support Cases process for:

- 
**Distributing** Support Cases that are escalated to R&amp;D to the respective owner team.

- 
**Handling** the Support Cases that are assigned to their team.&nbsp;

The developers assigned to the Support role are not fixed, this role is fulfilled by all team developers in rotation.

The child pages below have the objective of detailing the Support Cases process, describe the tools used in the process and R&amp;D&rsquo;s responsibilities.

Start by learning the Support Case process at a high level [here](https://outsystemsrd.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/RCP/pages/723845362/Support+Cases+High+Level+LifeCycle).
