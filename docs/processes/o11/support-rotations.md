# Support Rotations

The **developers** assigned to the Support role are **not fixed**, this role is fulfilled by all team developers in rotation.

We have a centralized system **RotationApp** to manage these rotations, so that:

- 
**Notifications** can be sent when Support Cases are escalated and need to be assigned to a team **only to the developers in rotation**.

- 
Everyone knows **who is in rotation in other teams**.

- 
We **reduce the likelihood of a team not having a developer assigned** to this role at any given time.

# Create a Support Rotation for your team:- 
Open the Rotations App [here](https://engineering.outsystems.net/Rotations/).

- 
Click the &ldquo;Rotations&rdquo; menu tab.

- 
Click the &ldquo;Add Rotation +&rdquo; button

- 
Fill up the information for you rotation taking into consideration:
- 
The Rotation name starts with the team&rsquo;s name

- 
The Slack channel is the team&rsquo;s private channel

- 
The Rotation Type is Support

- 
Number of simultaneous participants is the number of developers assigned to Support at the same time

# Define the participants in the rotation:- 
Open the Rotations App [here](https://engineering.outsystems.net/Rotations/)

- 
Click the &ldquo;Rotations&rdquo; menu tab

- 
Search for your rotation and click the &ldquo;Participants&rdquo; link

- 
Click the &ldquo;Add Participant&rdquo; link on the &ldquo;Actions&rdquo; plane

- 
Search for your team&rsquo;s developers and add them

# Create the first rotation schedule:- 
Open the Rotations App [here](https://engineering.outsystems.net/Rotations/)

- 
Click the &ldquo;Rotations&rdquo; menu tab

- 
Search for your rotation and click the &ldquo;Schedule&rdquo; link

- 
Click the &ldquo;New Rotation Cycle +&rdquo; button
- 
For the first time you&rsquo;ll be prompted to input:
- 
The start day for the first rotation

- 
The frequency (how long does each participant stay in rotation)

- 
We recommend the rotation start day matches a sprint start day and that the frequency matches the sprint duration.

# Notifications:
- 
**Participants** are notified via Slack 3 days **before their rotation starts**

- 
**Team** is notified when there are rotation **slots with no assignee** (Note that participants vacations are refreshed every day and as a result of that Vacations replacement slots will be created)

# How to know who from other teams is on rotation?- 
Open the &ldquo;Who&rsquo;s On Rotation&rdquo; screen [here](https://engineering.outsystems.net/Rotations/WhosOnRotation)

- 
Select the Date (Current Day is the default value)
