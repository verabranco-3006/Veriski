# Action Guide for Internal Communications Leader

noneaction guide - 1## Setup####  1. **Review the list of VS Leaders and Engineering Mangers**
Ensure the list of Value Stream Leaders and Engineering Managers is always up to date.

This list will be initially used to configure the Escalations Policies for each Team: 
- 1st responder: Engineering Manager
- 2nd responder: Value Stream Leader

####  2. **Set up your Rootly account**
Set up a Rootly account, and make sure you have the right permissions to:

- 
See the Status Page [Incident Response: Observer]

- 
Update the Status Page [Incident Response: User]

####  3. **Configure Rootly on-call**
All team members who could assume the role of IC Leader should have ***Rootly on-call*** configured, in order to receive notifications from Rootly:

- 
Ensure your phone number and email are correct    

- 
Ensure you receive notifications via your preferred channels and formats  

#### ** 4. Confirm your Team Escalation Policies**
Confirm that the **Escalation Policy** defined for your **Team** is correctly configured o Rootly. The Escalation Policies name follows the format: **IC Leader - &lt;Team Name&gt; - Team EP **.

The **Internal Communications Leader** could be the **Value Stream Leader, Engineering Manager, Team Lead, or another senior team member, defined within each Team.**

**Each *****Value Stream Leader***** is accountable to define how to address this responsibility - **either through direct delegation, rotation schedules or any other method, including assuming the role directly, as initially suggested.

If the **&ldquo;*****first responder&rdquo; does not acknowledge*** the notification, their **backup** will be notified according to the *Escalation Policy* defined.

 See more information here: Schedules must be associated with an Escalation Policy  

## After Incident Declaration#### ** 5. Acknowledge the notification when you receive it **
It is **very important that the notification is acknowledged**, to avoid notifying the designated backup.

 See more information here: Guide to responding to Rootly alerts.

SMS

Rootly App

Email
