## Next Meetings
Find below the schedule for the next meetings to discuss Emergency Changes Root Cause Analysis. All the Change Reviewers will be invited to participate. You can check the scheduled date on your RFC using the field &ldquo;**To be discussed in CAB Meeting**&rdquo; in the *CAB Review* tab.

Participation of Required Attendees is mandatory. If you are not able to attend, engage with Process Engineering to reschedule or delegate someone from your team to represent you in the discussion.

### RCA Discussion meeting Model
RCA discussions will follow a model similar to a master thesis presentation - a 30-minute session, with a recommendation to use 10 minutes to present the RCA and 20 minutes to discuss the RCA.

- 
**The Panelist (P) - **the Change Requester who submitted the Emergency Change and is responsible for presenting the RCA to the audience.

- 
**The Reviewers (R) - **members of the **Reviewers Pool**, assigned to review and provide inputs to the discussion and advise the approvers about the content and the business justification to the RCA. It is required at least 2 reviewers per discussion and segregation of duties must be granted, by at least 1 of the reviewers.

- 
**The Moderator (M) - **is** **responsible for ensuring the meeting is recorded, meeting notes are logged and action items are captured and followed up. This role will be played by the Process Engineering Team
