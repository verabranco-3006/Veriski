# How to deal with Problem Records that lead to Feature Requests

During the analysis of a Problem Record and once the RPM has reached the state Investigation completedBlue, you may conclude that the way to correct the situation relies on a new feature to be introduced in the OutSystems product.

Once that happens the following steps should be followed, after consulting the Team Lead for the corresponding team assigned to the RPM:
- 
If the Problem Record has a **workaround**, document it in the Jira issue.

- 
Change the **status of the Problem Record to **closedGreen and fill in the following fields:

- 
**Status Reason List:** Not Fixed with Known Error

- 
**Justify Status Decision: ***&quot;This is not a bug, but a new feature requiring further development on the OS Product. As such, it will be evaluated and if it is deemed valuable for a relevant portion of our customer base, it will be planned to be made available in a future version of the product.&rdquo;*

- 
**Link the RPM to any resource** (documentation, Jira issue, Product Board issue&hellip;) that may be useful in the future, related to the new feature that should be implemented in order to fix the problem.
