## What We Achieved

We reached agreement on critical operational changes across 4 areas:

**Operating Models:** Separate Service Incidents from Support Cases, communication handover to GS for O11, remove internal communication layer from incident process

**Collaborative Learning:** GS participates in Retrospective process, Problem Management as action item follow-up, all status page incidents require RCA

**O11 Cloud Operations:** GS autonomy for customer problems, catalog automation through Cloud Backoffice, SRE involvement when cloud changes needed, swarming mechanism similar to ODC

**Resolution Times:** OLAs as governing mechanism for Support Cases, GS enabled to resolve without Engineering escalation, regular Engineering-GS operational review (aligned to V2MOM 7.1)

## What Needs Further Discussion

**2. CritSits Engagement and RCA Alignment**
