### 3. Ownership Model — How We Work Going Forward

**Three decisions I make at submethod level:**
1. **What to work on next** — sequencing between submethods based on dependencies and priority
2. **Can we take more intake** — new requests evaluated against capacity model before accepted
3. **Are we on track** — submethod progress tracked weekly via SP totals

**What the team owns:**
- Task breakdowns within submethods (add, merge, split tasks as work evolves)
- Task-level estimates (2-5-8 scale)
- Task sequencing within the submethod timeframe
- SP Spent tracking after task completion

**Jira integration:**
- Epics = submethods (RSA-xxx IDs already mapped)
- Tasks = individual work items under each epic
- SP field and Start/End Date fields on both epics and tasks
- HTML views (roadmap, capacity, schedule) are the decision layer — Jira is the execution layer
