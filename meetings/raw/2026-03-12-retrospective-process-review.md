## Notes

**Problems Identified by João Rodrigues:**
- Analysis is currently based on volunteers or enforced approaches, not in the middle
- We do not invest on when we should
- We left dimensions of analysis out

**Major Shift Agreed:**
Retrospectives will move from SRE-led to team-led, guided by principles established on the Production Readiness Checklist and the Reliability Platform.

**New Collaborative Model:**
- Teams with assets causing the incident will lead the retrospective
- Collaboration with other teams including Process Engineering and Quality
- Teams own their retrospectives, guided by established principles

**Impact on Operations Review:**
The end goal will change how Operations Review is led in the future. Analytics will drive discussions per value stream:
- Detection ratio
- SLO coverage
- RCA lead time and quality

**Change in Paradigm:**
This change implies narrative recreations. Process Engineering will work together with SRE on enabling value streams and their managers to drive these changes.

**Narrative Shift:**
Retrospectives must recenter the narrative to be based on **outcome, not output**.

**Next Steps:**
- May need to create auditing mechanisms to include in Operations Review
- Will continue to report current data
- Process Engineering to enable value streams for this transition
