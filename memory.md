## Anti-Patterns

### Things I've Learned the Hard Way
1. **"One Woman Show"** — Being at the center of every high-criticality execution prevents team growth. Step back even when it feels risky.
2. **Execution Without Metrics** — Qualitative success is not enough. If I can't measure it, I can't improve it or prove impact.
3. **Say Yes to Everything** — Not every intake request deserves attention. Use 80/20 rule: courage to decline what doesn't align with long-term vision.
4. **Control During Crisis** — Tendency to take total control during Viamanta-level incidents. This is the exact moment to empower, not centralize.
