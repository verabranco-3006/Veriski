### Workshop Findings Reference

From yesterday's workshop, 7 challenges with detailed outcomes (updated from Confluence):

**Challenge #1: Operating Models to Handle Customer Disruptions**
- Problem: Service incidents vs support cases conflated, severity models misaligned, no CritSit protocols
- Impact: Delays for Premium Support customers, confused reporting for Engineering
- ✅ Agreed: Separate service incidents from support cases
- ❌ No agreement: Engineering 24x7 coverage for high-urgent support cases
- 🅿️ Parking Lot: CritSit definition moved to Challenge #7

**Challenge #2: Inconsistent Customer Communication on O11**
- Problem: SRE manages customer comms instead of GS
- Impact: Inconsistent quality, role confusion
- ✅ Agreed: Communication handover to GS (timeline TBD)
- 🔄 Needs alignment: Timeline with milestones, training requirements

**Challenge #3: Missing Collaborative Learning**
- Problem: RCAs siloed, no CritSit RCA protocol, unclear ownership for monitoring RCAs
- Impact: Delayed RCAs, inconsistent customer experience
- ✅ Agreed: GS participates in Retrospectives, Problem Management as follow-up, status page incidents require RCA
- 🔄 Needs alignment: Service incident criteria, OLAs for Problem/RCA moments

**Challenge #4: Service Incidents Failing to Communicate Effectively**
- Problem: Slow status page acknowledgment, slow post-mortem, IC Leader role redundant
- Impact: Communication delays, role redundancy creating toil
- ✅ Agreed: Remove VS/EM as IC Leader (SRE owns direct), review automations, deterministic GS incident declaration, single O11 customer can be service incident, communication as first-class citizen
- 🔄 Needs alignment: Notification from Engineering to GS for suspected impacts

**Challenge #5: Higher Resolution Times**
- Problem: Engineering bottlenecks for ODC incidents drive MTTR up
- Impact: GS SLA performance affected, Engineering overloaded
- ✅ Agreed: OLAs for support cases, GS enablement to resolve without escalation
- 🔄 Needs alignment: Which OLAs to implement, escalation targets per SSL
- 📋 Next: Regular Eng-GS operational review, release visibility for GS

**Challenge #6: O11 Operating Models for Higher Severity**
- Problem: GS escalates without warm hand-over, requests changes without incident escalation, blurred ownership
- Impact: Slow response, poor coordination during critical incidents
- ✅ Agreed: GS autonomy, Catalog items fully automated, automation assessment shared, SRE involved when cloud change suspected, swarming mechanism similar to ODC
- ✅ Execution Issue: ODC swarming needs review/fix
- ⚠️ Risk: GS concern on reactivity vs response times

**Challenge #7: CritSits Engagement and RCA Alignment (NEW - Split from #1)**
- Problem: No formal CritSit protocols, no 5-day RCA process, unclear weekend engagement
- Impact: Premium Support customer experience risk, churn risk
- ⏸️ Status: Requires dedicated Engineering Leadership session
- Proposed: Align CritSit definition, define SRE engagement, establish 24x7 coverage, define RCA involvement with 5-day SLA
