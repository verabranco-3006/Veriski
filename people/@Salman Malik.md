**4. Organizational Challenges (be direct)**

*a) Blame culture in operations*
- Ops Review is perceived as a blaming meeting — teams show up defensive, not learning
- Retrospectives suffer from the same dynamic — teams treat them as bureaucratic obligations, not learning opportunities
- This undermines everything Salman's org is supposed to build. If teams fear accountability instead of embracing it, the operating model won't scale

*b) "Fake" continuous improvement cycle*
- The organization talks about continuous improvement but the cycle is broken
- PE drives the front end: retrospectives happen, data is collected, patterns are identified
- But the output loop doesn't close — findings from retrospectives and Ops Reviews don't systematically convert into actions that teams execute and PE can track
- We are enforcing the process but the improvement outcomes are not materializing because there's no mechanism to ensure follow-through
- Ops Review should be where the cycle closes: data → insight → action → verify → repeat. Today it stops at "data → present → next slide"

*c) GS collaboration friction*
- PE needs close collaboration with Global Support — now under the same org (Paulo Garcia)
- Historical dynamic: defensive positioning over joint problem-solving (GSSA leadership focused on optics rather than root causes)
- Salman's org structure is the enabler to unblock this — PE and GS as peers, not external parties

*d) R&D reach and process scalability*
- PE has the perception of which teams underperform on process execution but lacks the confirmation mechanism
- No systematic routine to pinpoint which teams are not following incident response, change management, or retrospective processes with data
- We need visibility and authority to surface underperformance — currently PE identifies patterns but has no lever to drive change at the team level

*e) Capacity vs scope*
- Team of 3 covering all of ODC, with O11 unification potentially adding scope
- V2MOM has 6 methods / 28 submethods — structured and realistic but tight
- At what point does the org invest in growing PE to match the ambition?
