## The Story Arc

### 1. Opening
- Thank them for their time
- Brief intro: "OutSystems has been on a mission since 2001 to empower organizations to build custom solutions for complex, mission-critical use cases"
- Stats: #1 in customer satisfaction (G2), 16K+ agents built, 2K+ certified developers, 900K+ community members

### 2. The Problem: The Enterprise Gap
- **AI ambition vs. enterprise reality**: 90% of transformative AI use cases stuck in pilot mode (McKinsey)
- Companies trapped in "wait and see" with endless pilots and disconnected experiments
- **Why?** AI is probabilistic (variable outputs), but enterprise apps must be deterministic (predictable, reliable)

### 3. The Solution: OutSystems AI Development Platform
**Bridge the gap** by combining:
- Probabilistic AI (accelerate build phase)
- Deterministic framework (deliver trusted AI apps)

**Three key capabilities:**
- Rapidly build custom apps and AI agents for mission-critical functions
- Modernize legacy processes with AI that wraps and extends core systems
- Manage and govern full lifecycle on one unified platform

**Key products:**
- **Mentor** — AI-driven development (90% foundational structure)
- **Agent Workbench** — Enterprise agent builder

### 4. The OutSystems Advantage (The Hook)
Unlike standalone AI platforms:
- **Unified**: One place to build, manage, govern apps + agents (no agent sprawl)
- **Agile**: OutSystems Model (OML) = innovate at AI speed with quality + control. Fully integrated DevOps, one-click publishing, automatic dependency tracking
- **Enterprise-Proven**: Flexible deployment (cloud, on-premise, hybrid), built-in security, 24/7 support, 600+ partners

### 5. Customer Stories (Pick 1-2)
**Axos Bank** — 60% faster development, 75% reduction in defects, 85% decrease in rework. Built intelligent agent for log analysis and document mapping.

**Petrobras** — 500+ professionals, 400+ deploys/month, 48 mission-critical solutions in 8 months. Built "Raí" AI agent: days of audit research → 5 minutes.

**Bosch** — 500+ apps in 4 years, 85% reduction in assembly line ramp-up time using AI-powered factory planning.

### 6. Close
- "As a next step, I'd love to schedule an expert-led, personalized agentic AI workshop"
- Invite them to connect: outsystems.com/community, outsystems.com/events

## Certification Process

### Option 1: Group Session (Recommended)
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes

1. **Review pitch deck together** (15 min) — walk through slides, emphasize The OutSystems Advantage
2. **Practice round-robin** (20 min) — each person delivers 2-minute version of the pitch
3. **Feedback + coaching** (10 min) — Vera provides feedback, clarify questions
4. **Final round** (15 min) — each person delivers full pitch, Vera uses scorecard to certify

### Option 2: Individual Sessions
**Duration:** 20 minutes per person

1. **Review key talking points** (5 min)
2. **Team member delivers pitch** (10 min)
3. **Feedback + certification** (5 min) — Vera uses scorecard
