## Notes

**Solution Definition:**
Team owning the faulty asset will lead the retrospective in collaboration with SRE (Incident Commanders), Quality, Process and Dev Teams. Faulty-asset must be identified during troubleshooting by the Incident Response Team.

**Criteria:**
Incidents declared with system-wide impact (new model - Service Incidents SEV1, SEV2).

**Key Changes:**
- Eliminate mandatory readouts but Retrospectives will be audited against a defined framework
- Lead Time to complete retrospective will be a leading indicator

**Challenges:**
- Identify the faulty asset and assign the responsible Team (rely on Internal Communications motion to identify the RCA Commander, for now)
- We need to think about a solution when we drop the Internal Communication motion
- We need to define the Retrospective Quality framework to test against the retrospectives being produced
- Challenge - how to ensure we are actually learning from retrospectives? We need to check evidence of improvement in MTTR of that value stream, on detection ratio and on retrospective quality and action items

**Training & Enablement:**
- We need to train the teams on how to do Root Cause Analysis (we need to establish the framework to do so and audit the teams on their training outcomes)
- Long-term we need to check evidence on analytics that we are actually learning from the incidents
- Readouts initially will be transformed into working sessions to execute the RCA (consider selecting the teams with worst analytics - low detection ratio, higher MTTR)
- Define the RCA framework and create enabling materials
- Prepare enabling sessions

**Process Flow:**
- RCA Meeting will be used to discuss Findings, elaborate findings root cause
- Action items could be determined offline between the teams
- The incident channel is the coordination channel for the team until the retrospective is completed

**Retrospective Framework:**
The retrospective framework will analyse outcomes from the RCA exercise (if teams covered all the pillars of the framework - detection, troubleshooting and recovery, coordination).

**Control Mechanisms:**
Action to add to the plan - define control mechanism points for this process and internal OLAs from Process Engineering to monitor (e.g. time to start retrospective, time to RCA exercise, time to approve, time to action item completion).

Problem Management as leading indicator of action item completion. Process Engineering to monitor Problem Record indicators (define how).
